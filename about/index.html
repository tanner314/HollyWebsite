Hi, I’m Holly and I’m so glad you are here! I am a professional recording artist and vocal coach located in Northern California who specializes in working with kids and teenagers ages 5-17. I find so much joy in creating a safe space for my students to discover their unique singing voices and unlock their potential as musicians. Music has a way of expressing what words alone cannot and helps us to come out of our shells and be lights in this world.
